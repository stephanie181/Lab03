How to Play

Click a face-down domino to reveal it. If its total pips are strictly greater than the Target, it is removed. After more than half of the dominos have been cleared, each successful removal also reduces the Target total by 1. The initial Target starts with a total between 6 and 10. If a clicked domino is lower or equal, it flips back down. The first time you click a specific too-low domino, you get a warning and keep your life. If you click that same too-low domino again, you lose a life. Clear all 20 dominos before you run out of 5 lives.
