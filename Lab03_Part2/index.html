Midnight

6-dice game where players aim to achieve the highest score by rolling a 1 and a 4, then totaling the remaining four dice

Game Setup

How to Play:

  • Roll 6 dice. You must hold at least 1 die each roll.
  • You must hold a 1 and a 4 to qualify.
  • Your score is the sum of your remaining four dice.
  • Each player gets one turn. Roll until all 6 dice are held. Highest score wins!
